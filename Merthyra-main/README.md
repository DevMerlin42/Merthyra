# Merthyra

Merthyra is an Apple-native personal workspace for writing, organizing, and managing everyday work in one calm place. It is built for notes, long-form documents, collections, projects, tasks, calendar planning, files, and local workspace assistance.

The goal of Merthyra is to feel simple enough for quick notes, but powerful enough to grow into a full personal knowledge and writing system. Instead of spreading ideas across separate apps, Merthyra brings the main pieces of a personal workspace together: writing, organizing, planning, searching, and remembering.

Merthyra is designed around real files, local-first workflows, and a clean interface that works across Mac, iPhone, and iPad. Documents can be organized with collections and tags, files can live alongside your writing, and the built-in Merthyra Assistant is designed to help answer questions about your own workspace without needing to leave the app.

## What Merthyra Is For

- Writing notes, drafts, articles, ideas, and long-form documents
- Organizing work with collections, tags, and projects
- Managing tasks, deadlines, and calendar items
- Keeping related files close to your writing
- Searching and understanding your personal workspace
- Building a private, organized place for your thoughts and work

Merthyra is currently in active development, with the first beta focused on stability, core writing workflows, iCloud Drive file behavior, mobile support, and making the app feel clear and easy to use across Apple devices.
