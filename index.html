Why switch to Merthyra?

One workspace instead of five apps.

Merthyra is built for people who want their writing, planning, files, tasks, research, and ideas to live in one calm workspace. Instead of jumping between separate places for notes, documents, reminders, files, and project planning, Merthyra brings your personal work together in a way that feels simple, private, and easy to grow over time.

I

One place for your whole workspace

Write documents, organize collections, manage projects, save files, track tasks, plan calendar events, and keep research together without rebuilding your workflow every time your work gets bigger.

II

Built around real files

Merthyra is designed around your own workspace files in iCloud Drive, so your documents and important material stay easy to understand, easy to sync, and easy to keep with you across your Apple devices.

III

Private by design

Your workspace is yours. Merthyra keeps the assistant focused inside your own app data, documents, files, tasks, calendar events, and saved memories.

IV

Simple when you start, powerful as you grow

Begin with one document and nothing else. As your work grows, Merthyra gives you collections, projects, tasks, calendar planning, research tools, files, and assistant support without ever feeling overwhelming.

V

Made for writing and real life

Not just polished final drafts. Messy ideas, plans, notes, recipes, projects, deadlines, research, screenshots, personal documents all the little pieces that become something bigger.

VI

Your assistant lives in your workspace

The Merthyra Assistant helps you search, organize, remember, and act on what's already inside your workspace. Ask about your notes, your next task, your saved files, or what you were working on last.

Merthyra Assistant

An assistant that knows your workspace and stays in it.

Ask about your notes, documents, tasks, calendar, projects, files, and saved memories. It responds naturally, acts on supported app actions, and never browses the internet or reaches outside the app.

Works with Siri & Shortcuts too: create a note, add a task, schedule an event, or spin up a collection with App Intents without opening the full app. Supports Apple Intelligence features like Apple Foundation Models and Image Playground where available.

Privacy & storage

Local-first. Yours by default.

No hidden databases, no cloud-only lock-in. Your workspace is real files you can see, keep, and take with you.

A local-first workspace

Your core data is stored as real app files inside your Merthyra iCloud Drive folder not a hidden CloudKit database. The core workspace works locally, like a real local app should.

Synced by iCloud Drive

iCloud Drive keeps your own Apple devices reading and writing from the same place, so your Mac, iPhone, and iPad stay connected to one writing system.

An assistant that stays inside

The Merthyra Assistant works with your local workspace context documents, tasks, events, files, and memories. It does not browse the internet or reach outside the app.

Everywhere you write

One workspace, across your Apple devices.

A markdown-friendly writing workflow for notes, scripts, research, and longform ideas on the Mac, iPhone, and iPad, with the iPad experience built to feel closer to the Mac while still fitting touch.
